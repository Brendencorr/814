Personalize your Clarity

Clarity is about distance traveled, not distance from perfect. Choose what showing up looks like for you - only what you pick counts toward your score.

What counts as showing up
Movement
Any activity - a walk counts
Your habits
The small daily things you're building
Reflection
A note, a journal line, a check-in thought
Your program
Progress in a Riley program you're in
Getting outside
Time outdoors, sun on your face
Staying connected
Reaching out to another person
Nourishment
Track nourishment
Count eating well as part of Clarity. Turn off if food isn't something you want measured right now.

Note: if you're tracking sobriety, that always counts - it's the foundation, and it's never something you toggle off.
