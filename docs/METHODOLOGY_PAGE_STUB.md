## How Clarity works (draft skeleton)
- Distance traveled, not distance from perfect: your score measures you against your own recent
  normal, never against anyone else or an ideal.
- Foundation (the basics every score shares) + Practice (what you chose to work on, banded to your
  own baseline) + Direction (which way you're trending against your own past).
- Missing days: your score fades toward "warming up" rather than punishing you; hard days widen
  the bands and never lower anything; a slip freezes your score while you repair, never craters it.

## The transparency line (canon - ships verbatim)
Clarity is an automated, algorithmic wellbeing signal - never a health measurement or a
diagnostic. It exists to reflect your own effort back to you, not to judge it.

## The Never List (canon - ships verbatim)
- Never weight or calorie tracking.
- Never medication tracking.
- Never chat-sentiment scoring - conversations with Riley are a confessional, not an assessment.
  A chat may invite a check-in; it is never itself one.

## Fairness stance (07 §14, publish as written)
Riley collects no protected attributes and will not collect them to audit itself - that would be
the worse privacy harm. Fairness here is architectural: personal baselines, individual
accommodations (customization, fuel opt-out, the hard-day flag), and individual-fairness testing -
identical behavior always yields identical scores.
