Questions
Before you decide.
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — one click, no phone call, from your settings. You keep access through the period you've already paid for, you're never billed again, and your account and history stay. You simply move back to the free Guide plan.

What's your refund policy?

If a Companion or Coach subscription isn't right for you, email us within 30 days of your first payment for a full refund. Individual programs and the Self-Guided Bundle ($8.14 / $18.14) are non-refundable — they're instant-delivery content you keep right away. Full detail on Help.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Nothing is deleted. You move back to Guide and your history stays exactly where it is. You can export or permanently delete everything anytime — see how we handle your data.

Does Riley remember me if I downgrade?

Your history is never lost. Long-term memory and the trackers pause on a lower plan and light right back up if you upgrade again — everything is right where you left it.

Why $8.14?

8:14 is the minute the light came back — the origin of this whole thing. The price is a quiet nod to it. The story is here.

Is Riley a real person?

No — Riley is an AI companion, and always honest about that. She was shaped by someone who's lived this, but she'll never pretend to be human. Here's how she works & keeps you safe.

Do I need a credit card for Guide?

No. Guide is free forever — no card, no trial, no timer. Ever.
