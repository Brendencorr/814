How Clarity works

Clarity measures distance traveled, not distance from perfect.

It is not a grade. It is a gentle read on how you are doing and which way you are heading - measured against your own life, never against anyone else's, and never against an ideal you are supposed to hit.

The three parts

Foundation - the basics every score shares

The honest basics, measured the same way for everyone: your steadiness (how you're feeling), your rest (sleep), and your fuel (nourishment - and you can turn that one off if it isn't helpful for you right now). This is the ground you stand on.

Practice - showing up, your way

The things you chose as your ways of showing up - movement, reflection, habits, your program, getting outside, staying connected. Scored against your own recent normal, so a good week for you counts as a good week - full stop. Focus lanes you opt into, like sobriety, live here too.

Direction - which way you are trending

Whether your last stretch is lighter or heavier than the stretch before it. Small, steady, in-your-favor movement is the whole point.

On the hard days

When you mark a day as hard, the bar widens - you cannot lose ground for struggling. Coming back the next day is what moves this, not being fine.

When you are new

For your first stretch with Riley, Clarity says "getting to know you" instead of showing a number. We would rather show nothing than show you something unfair. And in those early days it can only rise - it will never drop while we are still learning who you are.

Focus lanes, if you want them

Some things matter to some people and not others. Focus lanes are opt-in - you turn them on, and you can turn them off any time. If you track sobriety, we offer a sobriety lane inside your Practice: it's measured by density, not streaks, so a single hard day never erases your progress. During a slip or a hard stretch we hold your Clarity steady rather than let it fall - because reaching out and coming back is the work, and it should never cost you. Not everyone is in recovery, and Clarity never assumes you are.

What Clarity will never do

  • Never a grade, a diagnosis, or a medical score
  • Never tracks your weight or counts your calories
  • Never tracks your medication
  • Never scores your conversations with Riley - a chat is a confessional, never an assessment
  • Never compares you to another person
  • Never punishes you for a hard day
  • Never shows you raw math or makes you chase a number

Your check-ins and Clarity are yours. They are used to personalize your experience with Riley and are never sold or shared. Clarity is a wellbeing reflection, not medical advice - if you are in crisis, Riley will always route you to real human support first.
