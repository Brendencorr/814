How Riley handles what you share

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The plain-English version. You're often here at a vulnerable moment - here's exactly what happens to the things you tell Riley, in words that don't need a lawyer.

It's stored in your account - for you

Your conversations, check-ins, dates, and progress live in your account so Riley can remember you and pick up where you left off. That memory is the point - it's what lets her actually know you instead of starting over every time. It belongs to you.

How Riley "thinks"

To reply, Riley sends the content of your conversation to an AI provider (Anthropic), which generates her response and sends it back. Under Anthropic's commercial terms, your messages are not used to train their AI models. The reply comes back to you and is saved in your history.

What we will never do

  • Never sold. We do not sell your personal information. Full stop.
  • Never used for ads. We don't build advertising profiles or share your data for cross-context ad targeting.
  • Never a performance to be judged. Your hard days are data we use to support you - nothing else.

You can delete it - really delete it

From your account settings you can export or permanently delete everything, anytime. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal data. The one exception is safety: if there was ever a crisis moment, a limited, de-identified record may be kept for about twelve months for follow-up and legal reasons - never for ads, never sold.

The short version: stored in your account, processed by an AI to answer you, never sold, never used for ads, and deletable whenever you want.

The full detail

This page is the friendly summary. The complete, legally-precise version - every provider, every right you have, retention specifics - is in our Privacy Policy. Questions? support@meetriley.us.
