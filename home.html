Questions
Before you decide.
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes - one click, no phone call, from your settings. You keep access through the period you've already paid for, you're never billed again, and your account and history stay. You simply move back to the free Companion plan.

What's your refund policy?

Coach: full refund within 30 days of your original purchase (your first payment). After that, cancel anytime and billing stops immediately - no future charges - but payments already made aren't refunded. Individual programs and the Self-Guided Bundle ($8.14 / $18.14) are never refundable. Full detail on Help.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Nothing is deleted. You move back to Companion and your history stays exactly where it is. You can export or permanently delete everything anytime - see how we handle your data.

Does Riley remember me if I downgrade?

Your history is never lost. Long-term memory and the trackers pause on a lower plan and light right back up if you upgrade again - everything is right where you left it.
